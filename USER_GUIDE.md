# How to Use PerfiKlose

PerfiKlose is a fast, web-based tool designed to help you capture photos, compress them into compact files, pack them into a folder, and share them via email—**all without installing any application**.

---

## 1. Opening the App
To use PerfiKlose, open your mobile phone's web browser (like **Safari** on iPhone or **Chrome** on Android) and type in the web address provided by your system administrator.

---

## 2. Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Add Your Photos
Look at the floating action buttons near the **bottom of the screen**:
* **📸 Take Photo (White Pill)**: Opens your phone’s camera immediately. Snap a photo, tap "Use Photo", and the app will compress it and place it in your Processing Queue. You can do this repeatedly for up to 50+ photos.
* **🎛️ Gallery (Round Glass Button)**: Tap the 4-squares icon to select and upload multiple pre-existing photos from your phone's photo library.

*When your queue is completely empty, you will see an animated camera icon and spinning rings in the center of the screen.*

### Step 2: Review Your Queue
As you add photos, they will display in the **Processing Queue** glass card:
* **Green Badges (e.g., `-92%`)**: This shows you exactly how much space has been saved on a specific photo. For instance, a 5.0 MB photo might shrink down to about 0.3 MB (300 KB).
* **Removing a Photo**: Tap the small **✕** icon in the top-right corner of any thumbnail to remove it.
* **Start Over**: To delete everything in the tray and start clean, tap the red **✕ Clear** button in the queue header.

### Step 3: Check Your Batch Size
Look at the large blue **Stats Card** at the top of the screen:
* **Total Size**: This large number tells you exactly how much storage space your entire compressed batch will consume. Keeping an eye on this ensures you stay under typical email limits (25 MB).
* **Progress Bar**: Shows the total percentage of storage space you've successfully recovered/saved compared to the original files.

### Step 4: Save and Share
When you are ready to send the batch:
1. Tap the **Share** button located centrally on the **bottom navigation bar**.
2. **Save to Phone Prompt**: You will be asked if you want to save the photo batch as a ZIP archive to your phone's storage. Tap **OK** to save a local backup to your Files/Downloads folder. *(Tip: Don't forget to delete these older ZIP files occasionally to free up space!)*
3. **Choose Email App**: Next, your phone’s native share menu will pop up. Select your email app (like **Gmail**, **Mail**, or **Outlook**).
   - *If using a secure link*: The photos will attach directly to a new email draft.
   - *If using a local file/link (fallback)*: The email client will open. You must manually tap the paperclip icon inside your mail app and attach the `Photos_xxxx.zip` file you downloaded in step 2. *(Note: Gmail on iOS heavily relies on this fallback!)*
4. **Send**: Type in the recipient's email address and tap send.
5. **Reset Prompt**: After returning to the app, it will ask if you want to clear the tray. Tap **OK** to empty your workspace and start a fresh batch.

---

## 3. Advanced Settings ⚙️
Tap the **Gear Icon** in the top right corner to slide up the Settings panel. Your preferences are saved automatically!
* **JPEG Quality Slider**: Adjust from 10% to 100%. Lower quality gives smaller file sizes. (60% is recommended for basic receipts/documents).
* **Max Resolution Slider**: Adjust from 600px to 3000px. This scales down the physical dimensions of the image before compression, preventing massive file sizes from 48 Megapixel mobile cameras.

---

## Quick Troubleshooting & Tips
* **Install as an App**: You can install PerfiKlose on your phone for easy access.
  - **iPhone (Safari)**: Tap the **Share** button $\rightarrow$ select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
  - **Android (Chrome)**: Tap the **three dots** menu $\rightarrow$ select **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**.
  - Once added, it will have its own home screen icon and run in fullscreen mode like a real app.
* **100% Offline Support**: After loading the app once with internet, it will work entirely offline! You can take pictures and compress them in remote locations with zero cellular coverage. The app will hold your pictures in its local database safely until you are back online and ready to share.
* **Auto-Saved Progress**: Your progress is automatically saved to your phone's browser as you work. If your phone goes to sleep, locks in your pocket, or if you accidentally refresh the page, your compressed pictures will still be waiting for you.
* **Stale Batch Warnings**: If you forget photos in the queue for more than 5 days, a yellow warning banner will appear at the top of the app to remind you to send or clear them.
* **Keep Screen On**: While photos are actively compressing, a warning banner will appear reminding you not to lock your screen. If your phone goes to sleep, compression pauses until you wake it back up.

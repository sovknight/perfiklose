# How to Install PerfiKlose on Your Phone

PerfiKlose can be installed as a standalone app on your iPhone or Android phone. Once installed, it runs fullscreen like a native app, launchable directly from your home screen, and works 100% offline!

---

## 📱 iPhone Instructions (iOS 17 & iOS 18+)

### Method 1: Using Apple Safari (Recommended)
1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone.
2. Go to the PerfiKlose web address.
3. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom center of the screen (the square icon with an arrow pointing upward ⬆️).
4. Scroll down through the menu options and tap **Add to Home Screen** (look for the square icon with a plus sign `+`).
5. (Optional) Confirm or edit the app name "PerfiKlose".
6. Tap **Add** in the top-right corner.
7. Close Safari—you will now see the **PerfiKlose** app icon directly on your iPhone home screen!

### Method 2: Using Google Chrome on iPhone
1. Open **Chrome** on your iPhone.
2. Go to the PerfiKlose web address.
3. Tap the **Share** icon near the top-right of the address bar (the square with an arrow pointing up ⬆️).
4. Scroll down the share menu and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Tap **Add** in the upper-right corner.

---

## 🤖 Android Instructions (Android 14 & Android 15+)

### Method 1: Using Google Chrome (Recommended)
1. Open **Google Chrome** on your Android device.
2. Go to the PerfiKlose web address.
3. If a pop-up banner appears at the bottom saying *"Add PerfiKlose to Home screen"*, tap it.
4. If no banner appears: Tap the **three vertical dots (⋮)** in the top-right corner of Chrome.
5. Tap **Install app** (or **Add to Home screen**).
6. Tap **Install** in the confirmation popup.
7. The **PerfiKlose** app icon will now appear in your Android App Drawer and Home Screen!

### Method 2: Using Samsung Internet (Samsung Galaxy Devices)
1. Open **Samsung Internet** on your Samsung phone.
2. Go to the PerfiKlose web address.
3. Tap the **download arrow (⬇️)** next to the web address bar (if visible), OR tap the **Menu icon (☰)** in the bottom-right corner.
4. Tap **Add page to** and select **App screen** (or **Home screen**).
5. Tap **Install**.

---

## 💡 Troubleshooting & Notes
* **Launch Fullscreen**: Always open PerfiKlose from your Home Screen icon rather than inside browser tabs for the best, fullscreen experience.
* **Offline Access**: Once installed, you do not need cell service or Wi-Fi to snap and compress photos. All images compress locally on your phone.
* **App Updates**: When a new update is released, a blue **"Update"** banner will automatically appear at the top of the app. Simply tap **Update** to refresh to the latest version instantly.
